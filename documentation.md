### Carleton Place Water Dragons Swim Team Volunteer Tracker ###

By the requests of Ms. Gilby during the Grade 12 level senior Computer Science (ICS4U) our team (Raymond Liu, Zilin Liu, Mary Liu) have taken on the tasks to build a comprehensive volunteer hours/money/supplies/contributions tracking system to modernize the old, inefficient, and paper/email based system that Carleton Place Water Dragons (From now on will be referred as "CPWD"). The planning stage of app determined the general layout and sections of the app and the two distinct portals based on roles of the user: Volunteer vs. Admin. Each sections of each portals will be outlined below

-Volunteer
--Main
---Tutorial
--Account
--Events (Sign up)
--Log (Log contributions)

-Organizer
--Manage
---Emailing (Send, schedule emails for volunteers)
--volunteers (add/edit volunteers)
---Volunteer lists
--Events (currently placed under the main page of organizer, will make a new main page for admin soon)
